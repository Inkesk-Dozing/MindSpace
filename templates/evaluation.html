What these numbers mean for student burnout
  • Precision — of all students we flagged as "High Risk", how many genuinely were? A low precision means false alarms, wasted counselling resources.
  • Recall — did we actually catch the students who were at risk? A low recall means students slipping through undetected.
  • F1 — the balance point. In welfare contexts, high recall matters more than precision; missing a crisis is worse than a false positive.
